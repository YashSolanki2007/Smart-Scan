What is Smart Health?

Smart Health is a facility that allows people to get information about the ongoing COVID pandemic. It also allows them to do Medical Scans on images of lungs to diagnose COVID-19 for free. We use a 96% accurate Neural Network to predict on the lung images which is more accurate than standard RTPCR reports. On extensive testing it was found out that the model got 3 predictions incorrect from a total of 150 images. It also has an AI Chatbot that informs you about various facts about COVID. And has all the current news about vaccines that you can also download so you can access it offline
